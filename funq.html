FunQ Mobile Application

My team and I designed and prototyped a mobile application that helps traveling workers on the road find local restaurants. Our design goal was to immediately provide the closest listings, and allow users to refine their criteria later if they so choose. I contributed by brainstorming design ideas, creating wireframes, creating a persona and writing a scenario of use. Advisor: John Zimmerman, Group Members: Joanna Bresee, Yash Vora, and Carlos Guiterrez

Shows current location and lists nearby restaurants based on saved preferences

Click on a listing to view details and get directions

Change preferences to view different listings

Persona

I created a persona and shot photographs of him in context in order to help drive our design ideation. Scott is a 27 year old camera operator who travels from city to city to film political events for cable. When he gets off work in a new town he wants to find a place nearby to eat and get a beer with his coworkers
